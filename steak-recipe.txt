Ingredients:
 - 1 steak
 - More than enough of oil
 - A pinch of pepper
 - Salt

Directions:
  1. Pour oil in the pan, and heat the pan till very hot.
  2. Put the steak into the pan. Flip it every 15 or so seconds.
  3. Use a temperature gauge to measure the inner temperature. 
  	- 50 degree: medium rare
  	- 55 degree: medium
  	- 60 degree: well done
  4. Take out the steak, and let it rest on a plate for 5 minutes. (Crucial Step!)
  5. Add pepper and salt as much as you like.
  6. Enjoy!
